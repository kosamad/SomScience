Discover

Our interactive and accessible exhibits are themed into four areas: The Human Body, Plants and the Environment, Space and Materials and Movement. We've also got seasonal live demonstrations showing science in action! These run mutliple times a day including one SEN modified show. If tinkering is your thing then come and play in The Workshop where we have different hands-on activities set up to spark curiosity. Come and explore with us!

Labs

Can you help us answer our Question of the Day? Join our lead scientist in the lab to undertake your very own experiments and find out! We've got four sessions each day to accomodate different age groups. Our sessions include "Who Done It?" "What Is This Made Of" and "How Do Rockets Fly". Sign up at reception.

Cafe & Shop

Re-fuel your body in our cosy cafe. Serving drinks, snacks and lunch all day, we've got something for everyone and accomodate all dietary requirements. If you want to take the fun home with you then take a look in our shop. It has everything you could want to continue your scientfic adventure. Or perhaps you want to give the gift of exploration to someone else? Find it near the exit.

Quiet Space

Sometimes we all need a little break from the noisy world. Our queit space is full of sofas, cushions and beanbags to relax on, with books to read and headphones which can play a variety of chilled out music or calm audio stories.
